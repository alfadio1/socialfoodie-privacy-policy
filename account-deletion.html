SocialFoodie Account Deletion Instructions

SocialFoodie allows users to permanently delete their account and associated data at any time. You may request deletion directly in the app or using the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account in two ways:

1. Delete Account Through the App

Inside the SocialFoodie app:

  • Go to Profile
  • Tap More
  • Scroll down and Select Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion
This will immediately delete your profile and all associated data stored in our systems.

2. Request Account Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion manually, you may email us at:
alfatogue@gmail.com

Use the subject line: "SocialFoodie Account Deletion"

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account information (email, name)
  • User profile data
  • Uploaded photos
  • Saved items and preferences
  • Any activity associated with the account

Data That May Be Retained

SocialFoodie does not retain personal data after deletion. However, technical logs (non-identifiable) may remain on our servers for up to 30 days for security and anti-fraud purposes.
